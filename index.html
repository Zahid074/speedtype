About SpeedType

SpeedType is a five-stage typing trainer. Each level raises the bar on speed, accuracy, and text difficulty — clear Level 5 and you're certified Pro. Each level also draws from a small pool of passages, so reloading or retrying a level may give you a different piece of text at the same difficulty.

How a run works

1
Pick a level from the keycaps at the top. Levels unlock in order as you clear each one.
2
Click anywhere on the passage to focus it, then start typing. The timer starts on your first keystroke — not before.
3
Matched characters turn white, mistakes turn red and underlined. You can keep typing past a mistake; it's still counted against your accuracy.
4
Finish the passage to see your WPM, accuracy and time. Meet the level's target to unlock the next one.
5
Clear Level 5's target and the PRO badge is yours for this session.

How scoring works

Words per minute uses the standard typing-test convention of one "word" = 5 characters:

WPM = (correct characters ÷ 5) ÷ (time in minutes)
Accuracy = correct characters ÷ total characters typed × 100

Only your first keystroke over each character position counts toward accuracy — retyping a fixed mistake doesn't erase the original error, just like on a real test.

Tips for a better score

  • Keep your eyes on the passage, not your fingers — glancing down breaks rhythm more than it helps.
  • Don't chase speed first. Accuracy above ~95% almost always produces a higher effective WPM than rushing.
  • Use Restart level freely — every attempt is untimed until your first keystroke.
  • Levels 4–5 mix in numbers and punctuation on purpose. Slow down slightly at symbols rather than guessing.
